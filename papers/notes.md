# Papers

## Jegadeesh and Titman 1993: Returns to Buying Winners and Selling Losers: Implications for Stock Market Efficiency
- Strategy: buy past winners, short past losers
- 2 behavioral explanations of momentum:
1. Investors overreact to news about long term prospects
2. Investors underreact to news about short term prospects
- See also: Jegadeesh and Titman 2001: Profitability of Momentum Strategies: An Evaluation of Alternative Explanations

## AQR: The Case for Momentum Investing
- 4 explanations of momentum
1. Slow reaction to new information
2. Asymmetric responses to winning and losing investments (disposition effect)
3. The "bandwagon effect" (overreaction, tech bubble)
4. Compensation for risk factor (but no factor has been identified)
